Make a perfect loop with your webcam.

Automate your Zoom Meetings

Unfortunately, Zoom will not accept videos in .webm format. Use cloudconvert.com to convert your looped video to a .mp4 file.

Open up Zoom. Click the Settings icon. Then click 'Virtual Background.'

Click the plus icon, and select your video!

Cover up your webcam with tape/fabric/paper/etc. and you're good to go! Once you start or join a Zoom meeting, everyone else will only see the looping video of yourself!
